The Hero’s Journey: Lost in the Woods

You have picked: Option 2: The thicket.

Charles pushed through the thicket, the branches scratching at his skin. The air was thick with the scent of moss and decay. He stumbled and fell into a clearing, where an enormous, green-eyed dragon lay coiled around a giant tree. The dragon’s wings twitched at his approach, but it didn’t seem hostile.
"I’ve been waiting," the dragon said softly, its voice filled with age and wisdom. "The witch sent you, didn’t she? I know what she’s done to you."
The dragon explained that he was a guardian of the forest, cursed by the witch long ago. If Charles could break the curse, the dragon would help him find his way home.

Option 1: Try to break the curse and become the dragon’s friend.

Option 2: Refuse to help and leave the dragon behind.
