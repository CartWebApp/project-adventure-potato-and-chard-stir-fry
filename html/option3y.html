The Hero’s Journey: Lost in the Woods

You have Picked Option 3: Refuse the gift, questioning whether you truly deserve to return home

The whispers grow louder, and Charles falters. He begins to wonder if he even deserves to leave the forest—if he truly has the courage to face the trials. But after some time, he shakes off the thoughts and decides he must continue. The maze is frustrating, but he finally finds his way out.
