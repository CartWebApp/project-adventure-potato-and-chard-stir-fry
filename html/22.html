The Hero’s Journey: Lost in the Woods

You have picked: Option 1: The cave.

Charles entered the cave, the cold air biting at his skin. As he ventured deeper, the walls narrowed, and a low growl echoed through the darkness. Suddenly, he stumbled upon a massive creature, its glowing eyes fixed on him. It was a dragon, curled up on a bed of treasure, its scales shimmering like jewels.
The dragon’s deep voice rumbled, "You should not be here, little one. But, perhaps... you could help me."
Charles stood frozen, his mind racing. The dragon didn’t seem angry, just curious. "Help you? How?"
The dragon explained that it was cursed by the witch and had been trapped in the cave for centuries. Only a true friend could break the curse.

Option 1: Help the dragon break the curse.

Option 2: Try to escape the cave and avoid the dragon.
