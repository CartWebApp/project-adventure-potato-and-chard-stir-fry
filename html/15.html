The Hero’s Journey: Lost in the Woods

You have picked: Option 2: Continue forward, staying focused and not getting distracted by the light.

As Charles ventures deeper into the woods, he stumbles upon something—or someone—unexpected.

Option 1: As Charles follows the light, he suddenly hears a low growl. From the shadows, a large, gleaming dragon steps forward. Its amber eyes lock with his, and it speaks in a deep voice. “You are lost, young one.”

Option 2: After a few hours of walking, Charles comes upon an old, wise woman sitting by a campfire. She tells him the forest is alive with magic and that the light he follows leads to great power—but at great risk.

Option 3: Charles finds a small, magical creature—a fox, with fur that glows faintly. The fox leads him to a hidden path and offers advice on how to find the source of the light.
