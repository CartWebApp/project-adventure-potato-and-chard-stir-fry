The Hero’s Journey: Lost in the Woods

You have picked: “I’m afraid not.” says the creature, “You must choose on or the other as these worlds are far too different from each other.”

At the edge of the forest, the creature stops and looks at you one last time. "You have learned much, and now you must make the final choice: return to your world as a boy, or leave it behind and stay here, in the forest of your new destiny."

Option 1: Return home to your village, ready to face the life you once knew.

Option 2: Stay in the forest, embracing your new life among the creatures and mysteries.

Option 3: Ask the creature if there is a way to do both, balancing the world you know and the world you have discovered.
