The Hero’s Journey: Lost in the Woods

You have picked: Option 1: You decide that you want to live here.

It’s afternoon and you’re sitting next to the lake, thinking how great your time has been here and you. have decided that you will stay here. You get up and go to Lucas’s mom and tell her, “I’ve had a wonderful time being here and, you know, I don’t have a real family back at my home. So, do you think that I could live here with you guys?” “Of course you can!”, Lucas’s mom says happily, “I’ve never seen Lucas have this much fun with anyone. You can definitely stay here.” When you hear those words, you feel a rush of excitement going through you . This is the happiest you’ve ever been.
