The Hero’s Journey: Lost in the Woods

You have picked: Reaction: You hesitate, not wanting to leave the dragon and the realm you’ve come to love.
Consequence: The dragon warns you that staying could mean being trapped in this world forever. You must choose: stay or return to your world.

You return home, but the memories of the dragon and the realm linger in your heart. You are forever changed by the journey you took and the bond you formed with a creature of legend. Though you have returned to your world, the adventures you had in the realm are a part of you now. You’ll always carry the lessons learned from the dragon, knowing that when the time is right, the veil between worlds may open again.
