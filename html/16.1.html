The Hero’s Journey: Lost in the Woods

You have picked: Option 1: As Charles follows the light, he suddenly hears a low growl. From the shadows, a large, gleaming dragon steps forward. Its amber eyes lock with his, and it speaks in a deep voice. “You are lost, young one.”

Charles has met the dragon, and the forest’s magic begins to reveal itself.

Option 1: The dragon, named Yren, tells Charles that he has been chosen to witness the ancient magic of the forest. It offers to guide him if Charles proves his courage.

Option 2: Yren looks at Charles with suspicion. “You are not the first to seek the light. You must first prove you are worthy.” Charles is tasked with a dangerous trial to show his resolve.

Option 3: The dragon does not speak, but Charles feels an overwhelming sense of connection to it. He follows its movements, trusting in its silent guidance deeper into the forest.
