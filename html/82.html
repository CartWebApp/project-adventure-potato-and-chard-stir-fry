The Hero’s Journey: Lost in the Woods

You have picked: Option 1: “Oh! I’m Charles. I come from the village right outside of the forest that way and I’m trying to get back. What’s your name?” (You point to where you think your home is).

“I’m Lucas Wilkinson”, he says excitedly “I’ve never met anyone from outside of here! Here, lets meet my parents, they might know where to go.” He takes you to his house. “Oh, who is this Lucas?” His mom asks. “He says that he’s from a village from over there.” Lucas replies. His mom looks towards you, “What are you doing out here this late?”

Option 1: “I was exploring the woods and got lost. Do you happen to know how I can get back home?”

Option 2: “That doesn’t matter. Do you have any way for me to get home?”
